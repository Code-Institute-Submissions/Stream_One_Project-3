What's on at Blasé Music Club?

Join us on 27th May Bank Holiday Weekend as we present a day of idiosyncratic musical performances, art installations and DJ sets in the great arches of Kachette, Shoreditch.

Two dance floors, afrobeat, jazz & disco.

Venue

Kachette, 347 Old St, London EC1V 9LP.

Travel

Nearest tube and train stations are: Old Street (Northern Line), Liverpool Street (Central, Circle, Hammersmith & City and Metropolitan line), Shoreditch High Street station Overground.

There are buses running from Liverpool Street Station to the venue as well as from Old Street, otherwise it's a short walk from either.

Mo Kolours

Anglo-Mauritian producer, singer and percussionist Joseph Deenmamode, aka Mo Kolours, effortlessly weaves Hip hop, dub, soul and other electronic iterations to create tasty Erykah Badu & Theo Parish-esque grooves.

https://mokolours.bandcamp.com/

Wu-Lu

South London multi-instrumentalist & producer Wu-Lu joins us with his full band line-up, having drawn our attention with his jazz driven work with SAMPLED a few years ago.

https://soundcloud.com/wu-lu

Penya

Gilles Peterson recently named this 5 piece on BB6 Music as one of the 6 acts he's buzzing about and for good reason. Penya bring mighty afro-cuban grooves hot off the release of their sensational Super Liminal LP.

https://onthecornerrecords.bandcamp.com/album/super-liminal

ENGINE

ENGINE have built upon a live reputation which includes supports for Connan Mockasin, Infinite Bisous and C Duncan. The band have received plenty of radio play from BBC, Radio X, NTS and more.

https://soundcloud.com/the-engine-1

ATHOS

With a strong emphasis on improvisation, Athos' ‘Alternative Folk’ sound is quietly developing its clasps, into a force to be reckoned with.

https://soundcloud.com/athosband
